Roles
#####

.. role:: Quality Manager
   :id: rl__quality_manager
   :status: valid

   The quality managers shall be responsible for the planning and coordination of the quality activities, i.e. the quality management. He/She shall lead and monitor the quality relevant activities of the project.


   Required skills

   * Degree: Bachelor degree in quality management, or similar technical/economical degree, or comparable work experience
   * Solid understanding of quality management
   * Knowledge in project management
   * Deep understanding of quality criteria and the correlating methods and procedures to achieve and verify them
   * Technical know-how of embedded systems

   Knowledge of standards

   * ISO 9001
   * ASPICE

   Experience

   * 2 years of experience in quality management
   * Experience in managing projects

   Responsibility

   * Creating/maintain the Quality Management Plan
   * Quality related project status reporting
   * Monitoring/improve of Quality activities
   * Coaching the project team w.r.t all questions related to quality
   * Execute Platform Process Audit
   * Execute Feature Process Compliance checks
   * Exceute Feature Work Product Reviews

   Authority

   * Escalation of planning topics to the project manager
   * Definition of quality assurance measures
   * Refusing the approval of work products as defined in the workflows
